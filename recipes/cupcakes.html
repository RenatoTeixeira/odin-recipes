Cupcakes

Description

These tiramisu cupcakes have all the flavor of tiramisu, but in cupcake form, including a coffee soak and a mascarpone frosting.

Ingredients

  • Cake flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Sift flour, baking powder
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter
  4. Bake in the preheated oven
